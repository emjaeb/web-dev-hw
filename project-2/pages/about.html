About Me

To keep things short...I love house plants!

My goal is to help you understand all of the in's and out's of being a plant parent. There is so much that goes into keeping a green household, and I'm always ready to help answer questions, make suggestions, or just ooo and ahh at photos of your thriving indoor garden.

I always shop locally when it comes to new green babies. The folks at Portland Nursery know me by name, and it's an unusual weekend when both Saturday and Sunday pass without a visit there. The best advice I can quickly give to those of you out there looking to start your own urban jungle: make friends with your local nursery! They will always be at your disposal for any questions you have, are happy to make suggestions, and will always be just as excited as you are about the new leaf on your Monstera.

Thanks for visiting, and happy gardening!!
